Forex - Currency Exchange Market

The foreign exchange market (Forex or FX) is the global marketplace for exchanging national currencies. It is the largest financial market in the world, with trillions of dollars traded daily. Forex trading is essential for international trade, investments, and tourism.

Forex is traded over-the-counter (OTC), meaning transactions happen directly between participants rather than on a centralized exchange. Key participants include banks, financial institutions, corporations, and individual traders.
