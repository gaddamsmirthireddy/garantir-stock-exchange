Commodities - Natural Resources Market

Commodities are raw materials or primary agricultural products that can be bought and sold, such as gold, oil, wheat, and coffee. They are essential for the global economy, as they are used in various industries to produce goods and services.

The commodities market is divided into two main types: hard commodities like metals and energy resources, and soft commodities like agricultural products. Prices are influenced by supply and demand, geopolitical events, and weather conditions.
